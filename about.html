About Tiki Soup

Simple ingredients, warm bowls, and comfort made fresh every day.

Our Story

Born from a love of comfort.

Tiki Soup started in a small family kitchen in 2018. We believe that a good bowl of soup has the power to heal, comfort, and bring people together.

Every batch is made from scratch daily, using only the freshest, locally sourced vegetables and ethical ingredients. We never use preservatives - just time, heat, and love.
